About Nitrate Loading

Nitrate loading represents the mass of nitrate transported by river systems, primarily from agricultural runoff and wastewater discharge. High nitrate levels can impact drinking water quality and aquatic ecosystems.

Key Information

  • EPA Standard: 10 mg/L maximum for drinking water
  • Sources: Agricultural fertilizers, livestock waste, urban runoff
  • Health Impact: Exceeding standards can cause methemoglobinemia in infants
